About Us

Sweet Treats Bakery is a family-owned business that has been crafting delightful desserts for over 20 years. Our mission is to bring joy to our community with every sweet bite. From cakes to cookies, we make everything from scratch using only the finest ingredients. We take pride in being a local favorite and love sharing our passion for baking with every customer.
